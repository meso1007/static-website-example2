What is
TSUCHINOKO


The Tsuchinoko is a mythical snake-like creature from Japanese folklore, known for its unique bottle shape and ability to jump and sometimes speak. Often linked to rural sightings, it's a humorous yet intriguing part of Japan's rich tapestry of legendary beings. It's also famed quick movements so nobody catch them.

Why is the name
TSUCHINOKO-BLOG

The name "Tsuchinoko Blog" is inspired by the elusive and mythical Tsuchinoko from Japanese folklore. Just like the Tsuchinoko is known for being hard to find, this blog aims to explore the culinary world incognito, providing genuine reviews without any special treatment. The essence of the Tsuchinoko's secrecy and rarity reflects the blog's mission to offer unbiased and authentic food critiques.
